CM Plan Site 1.0.1

This website Hosts a generic Configuration Management (CM) Plan.

The intent is to build and maintain a site where best practices for CM Plan management are stored.

Changes to this site are used to demonstrate and experiment with Continuous Integration and Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) best practices.

In addition, the repository used to maintain this information can be used to quickly establish an internal website for CM process management and training on a Virtual Machine (VM).

Initially this site covers best practices for CI/CD of coding, building, and deployment automation.

Deployment Procedure:

  • ~/[homepath]/CM-Plan-Site/app/bin/package-deploy.bsh M m i
  • ~/[homepath]/CM-Plan-Site/deploy/vagrant up

Patch Deployment Procedure:

  • Above Deployment Procedure executed at least once
  • ~/[homepath]/CM-Plan-Site/app/bin/package-deploy.bsh M m i
  • ~/[homepath]/CM-Plan-Site/deploy/vagrant provision
